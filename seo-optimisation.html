Search Engine Optimisation Services

Search engine optimisation (SEO) is the process of affecting the visibility of a website or a web page in a search engine's "natural" or unsponsored ("organic") search results. In general, the earlier (or higher ranked on the search results page), and more frequently a site appears in the search results list, the more visitors it will receive from the Search Engine Results Page. The higher ranked listing will typically generate more visits to that listing than lower ranked listings. SEO may target different kinds of search, including image search, local search, video search, academic search, news search and industry-specific vertical searches.

Organic SEO is not just about your company's website. It's about all of your sites that your customers are going to see when they're searching for something on Google or Bing. That's why we offer an organic SEO audit as part of our SEO service package — so that you can get an idea of what other websites are out there and how they're affecting your rankings in Google.

Link Building

This involves building links from other websites back to yours in order to increase its authority in search engines. Link building can also help improve the visibility of certain pages on your site. Link building is one of the most important aspects of SEO because it helps increase the trustworthiness of your site by showing Google that other reputable sites are linking back to yours. Think of it as a networking strategy among businesses of the same industry. We'll help build links from other relevant sites so yours looks more trustworthy in Google's eyes! RJW Digital focuses on natural link building to get your brand the recognition it deserves.

Keyword Research

Keywords are what drive people to your website, so it's important that you know which ones are most relevant for your business and industry. Customers know right away what keywords they will put in their favourite search engines, so it’s important that you as a business owner know as well what your target audience will put in their local searches. We'll help you find the right keywords that will bring in traffic from potential customers and lead them through the funnel of conversion into customers. We'll also research how competitive those keywords are so that we can ensure our strategy won't waste time or money on unprofitable terms.
