Search Engine Optimisation

Our bread and butter is Search Engine Optimisation (SEO). Think of the internet as this vast space where everyone competes for attention. It's like a loud marketplace that gets confusing quickly, especially for business owners who just want to get noticed by their audience.

 

SEO is quite technical work. It relies on looking at specific demographic data of your target market, researching their online journey, and looking at where they leave their digital footprints. You want to make sure that when you advertise your services, you make the right impression as if you were the solution or product they were looking for all along.

Consider your business already seen if your online presence uses the right keywords and phrases. You will be the first in line in search engines (like Yahoo or Google), and more traffic will be routed to your published pages when people search for anything related to your services. This means more eyes on your shop, even in a space as cluttered and packed as the internet.

When RJW Digital works on SEO projects, we operate on a deep framework that taps on multiple platforms all at once. We use highly-advanced software to get to know your target market better. Then, we also check out your competitors (including their social media platforms) and see how they measure up to the market. Then, we choose the best practices for your business to get noticed and appreciated. No stone is left unturned with us.

PPC Marketing Campaigns

Fast results matter. Many digital marketing services often charge their services on a project basis regardless of success, timelines, or budget. The problem with this setup? Many marketing campaigns don't make the cut. In some cases, click goals are not reached (meaning businesses are unnoticed), or audience engagement doesn't go as planned. But the business owners still get to foot the FULL bill.

That's where PPC marketing campaigns come in. RJW Digital offers pay-per-click marketing campaigns that only charge based on how many actual clients clicked on your marketing campaign. PPC is a digital marketing service that provides data on what works and what doesn't through A/B testing of different copy, content, style, etc. This means we can craft the best messaging and content as fast as possible with a dynamic measurement through PPC. You get more for less with RJW Digital.
